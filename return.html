Return Policy

We offer refunds or exchanges within the first 7 days from the date of your purchase. If more than 7 days have passed, returns, exchanges, or refunds will not be accepted.

1. Eligibility for Returns & Exchanges

To be eligible for a return or exchange:

  • The item must be unused and in the same condition as received.
  • The item must be in its original packaging.
  • Sale items may not be eligible for return or exchange.

2. Defective or Damaged Items

Only defective or damaged items are eligible for replacements upon an exchange request.

3. Exempted Items

Some products or categories may be exempt from returns or refunds, and these will be indicated at the time of purchase.

4. Return & Exchange Process

Once we receive your returned/exchanged item, we will inspect it and notify you via email regarding the receipt and approval status. If approved, the return or exchange will be processed according to our policies.
