Shipping Policy

Orders are shipped through registered domestic courier companies and/or speed post only. Orders are shipped within 10 days from the date of order and/or payment, or as per the agreed delivery date at the time of order confirmation.

1. Shipping & Delivery

  • Shipping is subject to courier company/post office norms.
  • The Platform Owner is not liable for any delivery delays caused by courier services or postal authorities.
  • All orders will be delivered to the address provided at the time of purchase.
  • Delivery confirmation will be sent to the email ID provided during registration.

2. Shipping Costs

If any shipping cost is levied by the seller or the Platform Owner, it is non-refundable.
