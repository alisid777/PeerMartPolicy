Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy outlines how you can cancel or seek a refund for a product or service purchased through our platform.

1. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations will only be considered if the request is made within 7 days of placing the order. However, if the order has been shipped or is out for delivery, cancellation may not be possible. You may reject the product upon delivery in such cases.

2. Exceptions

We do not accept cancellation requests for perishable items such as flowers, eatables, etc. Refunds or replacements are possible only if the user proves the product's poor quality.

3. Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please report it to our customer service within 7 days of receipt. The seller/merchant will verify the claim before processing the request.

4. Product Not as Expected

If the received product does not match its description or your expectations, contact our customer service within 7 days of delivery. Our team will review the complaint and take appropriate action.

5. Manufacturer Warranty

For products covered by a manufacturer's warranty, please contact the manufacturer directly for resolution.

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed within 7 days by Branon Innovations Private Limited.
